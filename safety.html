Aircraft Refueling Safety Precautions

Precautions:

  • Training: All personnel must be trained.
  • Inspection: Check equipment for leaks.
  • Grounding: Connect aircraft and refueling truck to prevent static electricity.
  • No Smoking: No smoking or open flames.
  • Engine Shutdown: Turn off engines during refueling.
  • Fire Safety: Have fire extinguishers and fire blankets ready.
  • Communication: Clear communication between crew.
  • Spill Containment: Have measures to prevent fuel spills.
  • Emergency Procedures: Know emergency plans.
